The Acetal Copolymer material has great impact resistance over other plastic materials. The use of stainless steel plates & pins increases the overall strength while maintaining corrosion resistance. The CC600 chains are intended for use in multistrand conveyors handling individual loads under conditions of mild corrosion. They are usually supported in channels and are highly flexible, allowing for fluid movement and flexibility when required.
