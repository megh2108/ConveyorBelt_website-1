Conveyor Roller

The uses for conveyor rollers include accumulation applications, reduction of product inertia, and high speed sorting. Drive conveyor rollers have rollers attached to a motor by a chain, shaft, or belt. We are manufacturer of a wide range of conveyor rollers which include Self Aligning Idler, Idler Roller, CARRYING IDLER and RETURN IDLER.
